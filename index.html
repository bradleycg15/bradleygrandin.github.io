About

My name is Bradley Grandin and I am currently in my final year of the Bachelor of Applied Science (Surveying) (Honours) at Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology.

I have been working within the surveying industry in a variety of areas including infrastructure, quarries, landfills, cadastral, laser scanning and construction projects. My main areas of interest include both cadastral and laser scanning work, with the variety of environments and projects being an appealing factor.

Education

Secondary Education: Graduated the Victoria Certificate of Education from Cheltenham Secondary College in 2017.

Tertiary Education: Currently studying fourth year of the Bachelor of Applied Science (Honours) (Surveying) at the Royal Melbourne Institude of Technology. Expected to graduate in October of 2021.

Work History

Landair Surveys: I am currently employed as an Assistant Surveyor at Landair Surveys whereby I conduct a variety of projects within the infrastructure, environment, property and resource industry. My responsibilities involve undertaking cadastral surveys, feature and level surveys, construction set out, laser scanning, monitoring surveys and GPS surveys.

ESO Surveyors: During my time as an Assistant Surveyor, I worked on the West Zone of the Westgate Tunnel project. My responsibilities included monitoring surveys, setout and as-constructed surveys and road conformance surveys. This experience exposed me to a fast paced environment whereby the necessity for precise and accurate work was clear.

Melbourne Golf Academy: I worked at Melbourne Golf Academy as a hospitality supervisor for 2 years. During this time, my responsibilities included money handling, balancing tills and receipts, opening and closing the facility, customer service and food and beverage service.

Skills

I posess skills in not only the fieldwork aspect of surveying, but also the soft skills required for successful workplace and client relationships.

Such soft skills include organisation, planning, communication, time management, teamwork and hospitality.

The practical skills I posess include Trimble survey equipment, LISCAD, Microsoft Excel, Trimble survey equipment and Interferometric Synthetic Appeture Radar (InSAR)
